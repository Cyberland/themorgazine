An Afternoon at the Party in the Park

By Jon Morgan

My kids and I arrived at around 2:30 p.m. There wasn’t a big crowd, but we could see that a few kids were taking their turn with the games, and that a bounce house had been set up. At 13 and 11, two of my kids were too old for the games, but I encouraged them to give it a try so that their younger brother, at 18 months, would have a chance. We checked in, and the kids received bags for the prizes, coupons for free ice cream, foam lightsabers, and a game card.

The Games

The games were pretty straight forward, There was a fishing game, one where you knock over a pyramid of cans, and another where if you hit the target hard enough with a sledge hammer you won a prize. Monkeys, I think.

The games were staffed by members of the Shepherd Maple Syrup Festival princesses and syrup queens.

Elijah

Kayla brought her baby brother, Elijah, over to the bounce house. It wasn’t very busy, so we helped Elijah inside and Kayla showed him how to bounce for a little bit.
